BEFORE YOU BUILD
Get set up first
1. Join the NextWork Discord
Hit “Interested.” Share your screen if you get stuck.
2. NextWork account
Sign in at learn.nextwork.org.
3. Explore the labs
Browse the available projects and labs.
4. Test Generate Project
Try the AI — describe what you want to build.
BUILD MODE
Ground rules
Progress over perfection
Get as far as you can. Done-ish beats not-started.
Watch the clock
You have ~80 minutes. Keep moving through the steps.
Document as you go
Screenshot each milestone — you'll need it later.
Stuck > 10 min? Raise a hand
Facilitators are rotating. Don't suffer in silence.
KEEP NOTES WHILE YOU BUILD
Capture these four
What I picked
The project and why you chose it.
What I built
The services and steps you actually did.
What broke
Where you got stuck and how you fixed it.
What I learned
Your one biggest takeaway.
▶ This is exactly what you'll share in the group round.
